PRADIPTA JATIS INDONESIA formerly known as PACTERA JATIS INDONESIA (PJI) established in 2014 as a joint venture company between Jatis Group and Pactera Technology Ltd. With believe in local resources capacity PJI support Pactera’s business in South East Asia, ANZ and Japan by providing all resources requirement to deliver world class IT Support and Services. Over the year in delivering a world class services to Pactera’s client, PJI expand their business by also focusing on local market capacity with a vision to become Worldwide Software Factory from Indonesia. To achieve this vision PJI empowered their resources with Java, .NET and mobile technology platform skills and also equipped them with the best tools, methodology, framework and business process in order to provide the highest quality of software. And that is precisely how we meet our customers´ expectations and ensure the success of their investments. In 2016, Indivara Group bought all PJI shares and change the company’s name into PRADIPTA JATIS INDONESIA, PT
